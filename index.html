Frequently Asked Questions

What are CAM charges in commercial leases?

CAM (Common Area Maintenance) charges refer to tenant-shared expenses for maintaining common areas such as lobbies, hallways, and parking lots, often reconciled annually.

How can AI help manage CAM charges?

AI lease tools automatically extract, track, and reconcile CAM clauses across leases, saving time and reducing billing disputes or missed recoveries.

Do these tools integrate with property accounting systems?

Yes, most tools like Leverton, Prophia, and Visual Lease integrate with platforms like Yardi, MRI, and QuickBooks for seamless financial reporting.
